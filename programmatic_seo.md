# Programmatic SEO Strategy for TasteBuddy

## Executive Summary

Based on the SEO analysis showing only 15.6 monthly visits and 3 ranking keywords, this programmatic SEO strategy targets high-opportunity keywords to potentially increase traffic by 300-500% within 6 months.

## Key Opportunities from SEO Analysis

### High-Priority Keywords:
1. **"all recipe app"** - 9,900 searches/month, difficulty 19 (VERY HIGH opportunity)
2. **"meal planner app"** - 8,100 searches/month, difficulty 50  
3. **"recipe app"** - 5,400 searches/month, difficulty 65
4. **"best recipe app"** - 4,400 searches/month, difficulty 42

### Current Status:
- **Traffic**: 15.6 visits/month
- **Ranking Keywords**: 3 total
- **Domain Authority**: Low (rank 54)
- **Backlinks**: 25 from 22 domains
